Study on Consumer Perception of Four-Wheeler Companies

Introduction

Statistics (Σ) is the science of data collection, analysis and interpretation. This project uses χ² (Chi-Square) and Z-Test to analyze customer behavior.

📊 Purchase Factors

From the study, most customers prefer cars based on Economic factors (34%) and Comfort (33%). Design and seat capacity have lower influence.

🏢 Company Preference

The analysis shows that Maruti Suzuki dominates with 42%. Other companies like Tata, Hyundai and Mahindra have nearly equal share.

Statistical Formula

Chi-Square Formula:

χ² = Σ (O - E)² / E

Z-Test Formula:

Z = (p₁ - p₂) / √(PQ (1/n₁ + 1/n₂))

📈 Results

Z = 3.79 > 1.64 → Reject H₀

χ² = 7.74 > 3.84 → Reject H₀
